Sweets Pack - Low Poly Series.
==============================

This package contains 54 ready-to-use low poly models of 15 kinds of sweets.

- Popcorn (600 tris)

- Pudding (84 tris)

- Tarte (176-397 tris)
  Variation: Berry, Blueberry, Cheese, Lemon


Optimized for

- PC
- Mobile
- VR


All models are in .fbx format.
All models use Only one material (1024 x 1024 texture in .png format).
All objects are optimized to have as few triangles as possible without losing quality and style.
